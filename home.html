Why Life on Land Matters

Original, action-focused reasons this goal is urgent for students and local communities today.

Restore

Protect & Restore Forests

Forests hold most of the planet's land biodiversity, yet we lose them every minute. Reversing this is something local tree-planting groups can start now.

  • Join a campus or community tree-planting day
  • Choose recycled or FSC-certified paper
  • Report illegal dumping in green spaces
Protect

Guard Biodiversity

Healthy land needs a web of species. Students can protect pollinators and native plants around their own neighbourhoods and universities.

  • Plant native, pollinator-friendly flowers
  • Avoid single-use products that harm habitats
  • Support local wildlife charities
Act

Heal the Soil

Fertile soil grows our food and stores carbon. Simple composting and mindful shopping keep land productive for future generations.

  • Compost food scraps instead of binning them
  • Buy from regenerative local farms
  • Cut food waste to ease land pressure
